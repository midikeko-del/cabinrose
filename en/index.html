Frequently asked questions

Is Cabin Rose Station halal?

Yes, Cabin Rose Station is owned and run by Muslim Bumiputera.

What are Cabin Rose Station's opening hours?

We open daily. Saturday to Thursday from 11:00 AM until 11:00 PM, and on Friday we open a little later, from 3:00 PM until 11:00 PM.

Where is Cabin Rose Station located in Kemaman?

We are at L 94-1, Jalan Raja Udang 3, River Front, 24000 Kemaman, Terengganu. Our cafe sits by the Kemaman river in the River Front area.

Can I book a table in advance?

Yes. Tap the Book Now button on this page and fill in the short form, or contact our crew.

Can I hold an event or birthday party here?

Yes. We handle banquets, birthday parties, official functions and buffets. Send the date and guest count to our crew and we will sort the space and the food.

What is the most popular dish here?

The Corndog Cheese Tarik with stretchy mozzarella inside is our best seller. Buttermilk Rice, Chicken Chop Grill and our homemade pizzas are also regular favourites.

What is the price range at Cabin Rose Station?

Drinks start from RM2.00 and snacks from RM7.90. Most rice sets and western mains fall between RM18 and RM26, while premium items such as the Argentina Ribeye Steak reach RM80.00.
